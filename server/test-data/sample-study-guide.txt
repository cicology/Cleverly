ADVANCED MATHEMATICS - STUDY GUIDE
===================================

COURSE OVERVIEW
This course covers advanced mathematical concepts including calculus, linear algebra, and mathematical analysis.

TOPICS COVERED
1. Differential Calculus
   - Limits and continuity
   - Derivatives and their applications
   - The chain rule
   - Optimization problems

2. Integral Calculus
   - Antiderivatives
   - Definite and indefinite integrals
   - Integration by parts
   - Applications of integration

3. Linear Algebra
   - Matrix operations
   - Eigenvalues and eigenvectors
   - Vector spaces
   - Linear transformations

4. Mathematical Analysis
   - Real numbers and properties
   - Sequences and series
   - Convergence and divergence
   - Functions of several variables

KEY CONCEPTS
- Understand the fundamental theorem of calculus
- Master derivative and integral calculations
- Apply calculus to real-world optimization problems
- Work with matrices and linear transformations
- Analyze convergence of series

EXAM PREPARATION
Review all worked examples and practice problems
Understand the derivations, not just the final answers
Practice applying concepts to new problems
